Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products in original condition with packaging. Electronics must be unopened or may be subject to a restocking fee. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you offer technical support for products purchased?

Yes, we provide free technical support for all products purchased from TechStore. Our support team is available via phone, email, or live chat during business hours to assist with setup, troubleshooting, and usage questions.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. We also offer expedited shipping options (1-2 business days) for an additional fee. Shipping times may vary during holidays or promotional periods.

Do you price match competitors?

Yes, we offer a price match guarantee. If you find the same product at a lower price from an authorized retailer, contact us within 7 days of purchase, and we'll refund the difference. Some restrictions apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and financing options through our partners. In-store purchases also accept cash.
