Our Story

Founded in 2015, TechStore began as a small startup with a big vision: to make cutting-edge technology accessible to everyone. What started as a modest online store has grown into one of the most trusted tech retailers in the region.

Our journey began when our founder, Alex Johnson, noticed a gap in the market for a retailer that not only sold tech products but also provided genuine expertise and exceptional customer service. He assembled a team of tech enthusiasts who shared his passion, and together they built TechStore from the ground up.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across the country, offering everything from the latest smartphones to high-performance computing equipment. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core values: integrity, expertise, and customer-first service.

Our Journey

2015

TechStore founded by Alex Johnson with a small team of 5 people. Opened our first physical store in downtown.

2019
